Introduction

General description and rationals of your project.

We want to make a rubiks cube program that allows the user to both shuffle and solve the cube. We also want to make it a bit more engaging by having the option of textures for the faces instead of just simple colours.

Problem solving and algorithms

One of the major things we are going to have to solve is how to rotate each of the cubes of the face together, rotating the camera around the cube and shuffling the cube in a way that makes it challenging for the player.

Design consideration

Our User will be able to manipulate the view directly, this would impact the data structures to reflect the users input, it would outut the new cube
The cube itself will be constructed of 27 intpendent cubes, they will be manipulated in clusters of 9
There will be 5 main Menus: Camera, Rotate, Shuffle, Texture, Quit
The Camera Sub Menu will allow the player to select to focus on 1 of 6 faces
The Rotate Sub menu will allow the player to rotate a 9 square unit: Top, Bottom, Left, Right, Front, Back
The Texture Option has a sub menu that contains different options for the cube to look
The Game will end with the user hearing a sound denoting they won, they can reshuffle to play again
